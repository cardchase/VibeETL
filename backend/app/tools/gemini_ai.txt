# Gemini AI Node

Connects your data pipeline directly to Google's Gemini Multimodal Large Language Models.

## Features:
- Passes text, documents, images, and audio directly to the LLM.
- Generates a new column containing the AI's response for every row.
- System Prompts allow you to guide the AI to extract specific schema patterns or insights.

## Parameters:
- **System Prompt**: The instructions for the AI (e.g., "Extract the sentiment from this text and return only POSITIVE, NEGATIVE, or NEUTRAL").
- **Target Column**: Which column from your dataset to send to the AI.
- **Output Column**: The name of the new column to append to your dataset with the AI's response.
- **Model Choice**: Choose between Gemini Pro (Reasoning) or Gemini Flash (Speed).
