The Sample Records node allows you to quickly isolate a subset of your dataset. This is highly useful for drastically reducing the size of massive datasets during the pipeline design phase, allowing the VibeETL engine to run sub-millisecond tests.

**Sample Methods**:
1. **First N Records**: Extracts the first N rows from the top of the dataset.
2. **Last N Records**: Extracts the bottom N rows from the dataset.
3. **Random Sample**: Extracts N rows randomly distributed throughout the entire dataset.

**Parameters**:
- **Sample Method**: The extraction logic.
- **Number of Records (N)**: The exact row count to reduce the dataset to.
